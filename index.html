This algorithm solves a simple monoalphabetic substitution cipher without assuming any space hints. There are plenty of sites that assist in a solution by providing frequencies, but this is a full solver (excluding punctuation, capitalization, identification of uncommon words). quipquip does the same thing with a different algorithm but has trouble without space hints. This algorithm uses a completely different technique. Enter the string to be decrypted in the box. Anything that is not a letter (spaces, punctuation) will be ignored. This is a proof of concept solver and by no means can decrypt all ciphertexts. Words less than 4 letters long and uncommon words will not be recognized. The algorithm is probabilistic. The example text will take around 500 iterations to arrive at the solution. Note that this algorithm may not get the full text or spacing right but can get very close.
